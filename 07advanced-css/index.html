Who we are

Rocky Mountain Anglers is a passionate community of outdoor enthusiasts and dedicated anglers who share a deep love for the pristine waters and breathtaking landscapes of the Rocky Mountain region. Our mission is to foster a sense of camaraderie among fishing enthusiasts while promoting conservation efforts to preserve the natural beauty and aquatic ecosystems of the Rockies. We offer a wealth of resources, including expert advice, guided fishing trips, and educational workshops, to help anglers of all levels hone their skills and connect with the great outdoors. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just dipping your toes into the world of fly fishing, Rocky Mountain Anglers is here to inspire, educate, and empower you on your angling journey in this magnificent part of the world. Join us in our pursuit of adventure, conservation, and the thrill of the catch in the heart of the Rocky Mountains.

The Rocky Mountain fisheries primarily cover the region of the Rocky Mountains in North America, which extends from British Columbia and Alberta in Canada down through Montana, Wyoming, Colorado, Idaho, Utah, New Mexico, and into northern Mexico. This expansive and diverse region is home to a wide variety of rivers, streams, lakes, and reservoirs that support a rich and varied fishery. Each state or province within this region may have its own specific regulations, fishing seasons, and species of fish that anglers target, but collectively, the Rocky Mountain fisheries offer some of the most sought-after fly fishing and angling opportunities in North America. .

What we do

  • Promote Fishing: They promote and encourage the sport of fishing, particularly fly fishing, as a way to connect with nature and enjoy the outdoors.
  • Education: Rocky Mountain Anglers often provide educational resources and workshops for anglers of all skill levels. This may include fly tying classes, casting lessons, and seminars on various fishing techniques.
  • Fishing Forecast: We will have a forecast of how the fishing is with our most popular lakes in the Rocky Mountains.
  • Reward for Merch: If you are wearing one of our Rocky Mountain Anglers shirts and you catch a Lake or state record while wearing the shirt we will give you a money reward.
